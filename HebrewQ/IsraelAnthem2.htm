Hope
Hebrew English translation
כֹּל עוֹד בַּלֵּבָב פְּנִימָה As long as in the heart, within,
1. Kol ‘od balevav penimah O while within a Jewish breast,
נֶפֶשׁ יְהוּדִי הוֹמִיָּה A Jewish soul still yearns,
2. Nefesh Yehudi homiyah, Beats true a Jewish heart,
וּלְפַאֲתֵי מִזְרָח, קָדִימָה, And onward, towards the ends of the east,
3. Ul(e)fa’atei mizrach kadimah, And Jewish glances turning East,
עַיִן לְצִיּוֹן צוֹפִיָּה, an eye still gazes toward Zion;
4. Ayin leTziyon tzofiyah; To Zion fondly dart;
 
עוֹד לֹא אָבְדָה תִּקְוָתֵנוּ, Our hope is not yet lost,
5. Od lo avdah tikvateinu, O then our Hope—it is not dead,
הַתִּקְוָה בַּת שְׁנוֹת אַלְפַּיִם The hope two thousand years old,
6. Hatikvah bat sh(e)not ’alpayim, Our ancient Hope and true,
לִהְיוֹת עַם חָפְשִׁי בְּאַרְצֵנוּ, To be a free nation in our land,
7. Lihyot ‘am chofshi b(e)’artzeinu, To be a nation where Moses led
אֶרֶץ צִיּוֹן וִירוּשָׁלַיִם. The land of Zion and Jerusalem.
8. Eretz-Tziyon viY(e)rushalayim. Zion, Jerusalem by even very few
